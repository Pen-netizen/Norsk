Norwegian Grammar Rules

This section covers some basic grammar rules:

  • Nouns have genders: masculine, feminine, or neuter.
  • The definite article is attached to the noun (e.g., "boken" for "the book").
  • Verbs are conjugated based on tense but not by person.
