Did You Know?

  • The Norwegian language has two written forms, Bokmål and Nynorsk. We'll focus on Bokmål, the most common form.
  • Norway is known for its beautiful fjords and rich Viking history.
  • The word "koselig" describes a cozy atmosphere that Norwegians cherish.
